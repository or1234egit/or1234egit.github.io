# Linear Algebra — Vectors and Matrices

This note covers vectors, matrix operations, and common identities.

## Sample

The dot product of vectors $\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}$ is:

$$\mathbf{u}\cdot\mathbf{v} = \sum_{i} u_i v_i$$
